## Sri Aradhyula

**AI Platform Engineering Architect** @ [Outshift by Cisco](https://outshift.cisco.com)

AI platform engineering architect with nearly two decades of experience building large-scale distributed systems, cloud platforms, and developer experience platforms at Cisco. Leading AI-native platform engineering initiatives that use autonomous agents to execute operational workflows, improve developer self-service, and reshape how software platforms are developed and operated.

Creator and principal architect of [Community AI Platform Engineering (CAIPE)](https://github.com/cnoe-io/ai-platform-engineering) — an open-source distributed multi-agent platform that enables engineers to delegate platform tasks to an autonomous AI platform engineer operating across cloud platforms, Kubernetes, DevOps systems, and enterprise knowledge systems. CAIPE is deployed across multiple platform engineering teams within Cisco including Outshift and Splunk.

- Active in [Cisco Open Source](https://github.com/cisco-open) and [Outshift Platform](https://github.com/outshift-platform) communities
- Speaker at KubeCon/ArgoCon North America, Cloud Native AI (CNAI) community meetings, and Cisco engineering architecture forums
